China Jiangsu PV power station is the first water surface PV power station of GCT Group. Photovoltaic modules are distributed above the water and aquaculture is performed underwater, with 30MW of generating capacity The water surface PV power station is located in wet environment, so that the dust is easily attached on the surface of photovoltaic modules, and dust and dirt seriously affect the power conversion efficiency, resulting in over 7% of decrease in the efficiency. To effectively remove the dirt on the surface of the PV modules, intelligent cleaning robot is used for regular cleaning, which can thoroughly remove dust and dirt on the surface of modules, in order to improve power generation efficiency. Two test have been taken, in order to see the efficiency of the cleaning robot.
